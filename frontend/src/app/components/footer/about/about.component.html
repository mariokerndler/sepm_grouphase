About our Service

Artsperience is the home of Austrian commission artists who love and thrive on the collaboration process in creating artwork personal to you. We launched Artsperience to challenge the industry to offer a fair and sustainable marketplace for artists, where anyone can discover and buy original art. We’re committed to eliminating the ‘starving artist’ syndrome and believe that affordable art and artist success shouldn’t be mutually exclusive.

It is the only service that allows you to find artists, facilitate art commissions entirely online and let you interact with artists so if you are looking to commission a painting, commission art or commission an artist, you came to the right place!
